01. THE FRENCH GIN

Welcome to the South West

The south-west of France has it all: the sun, the sea, the countryside...

and the château de Bonbonnet, which we are proud to call home!

Juniper trees grow wild across from the château. Their berries gave our founder Alexandre Gabriel an idea: to create the first modern French gin. It was a crazy idea back in 1996, the year Citadelle Gin was born!
